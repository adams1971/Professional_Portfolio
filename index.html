About Adam

My Background

First Career Passion

I am a frontend web developer with a rich background in hospitality management and teambuilding. Throughout my career the focus has been to provide guests with the best possible experience while positioning my organization in the community sightlines. Now, moving into a new career, the focus remains the same. As a recent graduate from a full stack web dev bootcamp from the University of Washington, I earned a certificate completing the course. I have developed new skills in HTML, JavaScript, CSS and Node.js with an emphasis on responsive web design. I enjoy working with a team that collaborates and shares experiences. Additionally, I enjoy figuring our challenges and problem solving - I want to know why! I believe in teamwork and asking for help, understand ing that teams get it done! Most recently I worked on a team project to develop a dynamic web application called WatchNext. This application ends boredom and helps users decide the next best show to watch on the web. In closing, I am looking forward to working with a progressive team where I can begin to use my new skills and offer my contribution
