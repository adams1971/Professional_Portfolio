My First Career Passion

I am a frontend web developer with a rich background in hospitality management and team-building. Throughout my career as an Executive Chef the focus has been to provide guests with the best possible experience while positioning my organization in the community sight-lines.

As a young cook, I was curious about the how gastronomy started, how were all these foods created? It wasn't enough to accept that food was just a necessity- there had to a reason. Along my journey, one major focus of my career has been in the creation of cured meats. From an early start, I found my self wanting to know what made them what they are? It was a long journey and learning the techniques was not easy! Making a great prosciutto or finocchiona is a detailed craft and takes commitment. This curiosity is what I bring to my new endeavor, a relentless pursuit to know why and how and of course to create!
